Let us know about Rose

Roses are beautiful and classic flowers that have captivated people for centuries. They have inspired countless poets, writers, composers, songwriters and artists over the years with their elegant blooms and contrasting thorns. It is often referred to as the “king of all other flowers” due to its beauty and attractive appearance. Roses come in a variety of colors, including yellow, red, white, green, blue, black, orange, and pink, each symbolizing different emotions such as love, friendship, and joy. With its rich scent and ornamental value, the rose is used in various ways, including in cosmetics, decoration, and as a symbol of emotions
